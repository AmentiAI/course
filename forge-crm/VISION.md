## The Problem
Crypto creators, NFT collections, and Web3 projects manage their communities
across 6+ platforms with zero connective tissue:

- Twitter/X DMs scattered everywhere
- Discord server with no CRM data
- Telegram group, no insights
- Holders tracked in a Google Sheet
- Collab opportunities lost in DMs
- No idea who their top 100 community members are
- Royalties + revenue tracked manually
- Whitelists managed in Excel
- Can't connect a wallet address to a Twitter handle

**Result:** Projects lose community, miss revenue, and burn out on manual work.

2. **Tracks every interaction** ‚Äî tweets, Discord messages, onchain activity
3. **Manages collabs like a sales pipeline** ‚Äî never miss an opportunity
4. **Runs campaigns** ‚Äî DMs, announcements, airdrops, WL management
5. **Shows your revenue** ‚Äî royalties, primary sales, mint revenue in one view
6. **Alerts you to what matters** ‚Äî whale buys, viral mentions, key holder activity
