## Pre-Flop Scenarios

### 1. **AA in UTG, 9-handed**
**Action:** Raise to 3bb
**Why:** Standard open, build pot with best hand
**If 3-bet:** Usually 4-bet (AA plays best heads-up)

### 2. **72o in BB, BTN raises 2.5bb**
**Action:** Fold
**Why:** Worst hand in poker, terrible equity vs any range

### 3. **AKo in MP, UTG raises 3bb**
**Action:** 3-bet to 10bb
**Why:** Strong hand, good equity vs UTG range

### 4. **55 in CO, folded to you**
**Action:** Raise to 2.5bb
**Why:** Playable hand in late position, can flop sets

### 5. **AQo in BB, BTN raises 2.5bb, SB calls**
**Action:** 3-bet to 12bb or call
**Why:** Strong hand but multiway makes it less valuable, 3-bet to isolate

### 6. **T9s in SB, folded to you**
**Action:** Raise to 3bb or limp
**Why:** Speculative hand, can see cheap flop or steal BB

### 7. **KK in UTG, someone raises UTG+1**
**Action:** 3-bet to ~12bb
**Why:** Second best hand, play for stacks pre-flop

### 8. **A2s in BTN, 3 limpers**
**Action:** Raise to 5bb (3bb + 1 per limper)
**Why:** Isolation raise, build pot with position

### 9. **88 in MP, EP raises 4bb**
**Action:** Call
**Why:** Set mining hand, fold to 3-bet unless super deep

### 10. **QJo in CO, MP raises 3bb**
**Action:** Fold vs tight players, call vs loose players
**Why:** Dominated by better Qx/Jx, but playable vs weak ranges

## Post-Flop: Flop Scenarios

### 11. **You raise BTN with AK, BB calls. Flop: K-7-2 rainbow**
**Action:** C-bet 60% pot
**Why:** Top pair, board favors your range, build pot

### 12. **You raise CO with JTs, BB calls. Flop: Q-J-9 two-tone**
**Action:** C-bet 40% pot or check
**Why:** Middle pair on wet board, be cautious but can continue story

### 13. **You raise UTG with QQ, BTN calls. Flop: A-8-4 rainbow**
**Action:** C-bet 50% pot
**Why:** Overpair but ace hurts, size to see where you're at

### 14. **You call BTN raise in BB with 76s. Flop: K-5-3 rainbow**
**Action:** Check/fold to c-bet
**Why:** Missed completely, no equity, board favors raiser

### 15. **You raise MP with AQo, BB calls. Flop: Q-T-7 two-tone**
**Action:** C-bet 65% pot
**Why:** Top pair top kicker, but watch for draws

### 16. **You raise BTN with A5s, BB calls. Flop: A-K-Q rainbow**
**Action:** Check
**Why:** Weak kicker in bad spot, pot control

### 17. **You 3-bet AA pre-flop, get called. Flop: 9-8-7 two-tone**
**Action:** C-bet 75% pot
**Why:** Protect vs draws, get value before board gets worse

### 18. **You call CO raise in BB with 44. Flop: Q-4-2 rainbow (you flop set)**
**Action:** Check/raise or lead out 70%
**Why:** Hidden monster, build pot aggressively

### 19. **You raise BTN with KQs, SB calls. Flop: J-T-3 rainbow**
**Action:** C-bet 50% pot
**Why:** Gutshot + overcards, good semi-bluff spot

### 20. **You raise MP with 99, BTN 3-bets, you call. Flop: K-K-5 rainbow**
**Action:** Check/fold to bet
**Why:** Bad flop for you, opponent likely has Kx or overpair

## Live Tell Scenarios

### 71. **Opponent bets quickly on river**
**Interpretation:** Usually weak (trying to look strong)
**Action:** Consider calling/raising

### 72. **Opponent takes long time then bets**
**Interpretation:** Usually strong (thinking about sizing)
**Action:** Proceed with caution

### 73. **Opponent's hand shakes when betting**
**Interpretation:** Usually strong (adrenaline from big hand)
**Action:** Fold marginal hands

### 74. **Opponent makes eye contact and smiles when betting**

**Action:** Consider calling
